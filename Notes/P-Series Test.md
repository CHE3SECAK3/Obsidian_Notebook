# P-Series Test
$$
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n^P} \rightarrow
\begin{cases}
\text{diverges}, &P \leq 1 \\
\text{converges}, &P > 1
\end{cases}

$$

> [!success]  P-Series Proof
> 
> For any value $P$, use the **Integral Test**.
>
