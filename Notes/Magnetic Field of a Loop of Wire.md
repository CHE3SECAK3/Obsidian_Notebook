# Magnetic Field of a Loop of Wire
> [!note] Magnetic Field Equation
> $$\begin{align*}
\vec{B}_\text{axis} &= \frac{\mu_{0}}{2} \frac{IR^{2}}{(z^{2}+R^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}}\\\\
\vec{B}_\text{center} &= \frac{\mu_{0}}{2} \frac{I}{R} 
\end{align*}$$
