# Resistance
- *Ability to impede flow of current*
- Symbol: $R$
- Units: $\Omega$


- Manipulation of Ohm's Law -> $R = \large \frac{V}{I}$ where:
	- $R$ : Resisitance ($\Omega$)
	- $V$ : Voltage ($V$)
	- $I$ : Current ($A$)
