# Density
- *The amount of stuff per unit space*

- $\lambda$ : Linear density $\rightarrow$ one-dinemsional space
	- $\lambda = \frac{m}{L}$

- $\eta$ : Surface density $\rightarrow$ two-dinemsional space
	- $\eta = \frac{m}{A}$

- $\rho$ : Volume density $\rightarrow$ three-dinemsional space
	- $\rho = \frac{m}{V}$
