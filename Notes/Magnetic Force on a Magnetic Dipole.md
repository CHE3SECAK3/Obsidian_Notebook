# Magnetic Force on a Magnetic Dipole
> [!note] Magnetic Torque on a Loop of Wire
> $$\vec{\tau} = \vec{\mu} \times \vec{B}$$
> - where:
> - $\vec{\tau}$ : Torque on the magnet
>
> - $\vec{\mu}$ : Magnetic dipole moment
>
> - $\vec{B}$ : External magnetic field
