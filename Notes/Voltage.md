# Voltage
- *Electric pressure*

- Units: *Volt* -> $V$

- $\large V = \frac{dW}{dQ}$, where:
	- $V$ = voltage ($V$)
	- $W$ = work or $\Delta PE$ ($J$)
	- $Q$ = charge ($C$)

- Voltage is the electric potential energy per unit charge between 2 points
