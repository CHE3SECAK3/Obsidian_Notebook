# Electric Field of a Disk
$$\vec{E}_{\text{disk}} = \frac{\eta}{{2\epsilon_{0}}}\left[1-\frac{z}{\sqrt{z^{2}+R^{2}}}\right]$$

where:
- $Q$ : Total charge of ring
- $z$ : Distance from center of ring to point of interest
- $R$ : Radius of ring
