# Current Density
- *The amount of current per area of wire*

- Symbol : $J$

- Units : $\frac{A}{m^{2}}$ where:
	- $A$ : Amps
	- $m^{2}$ : Cross-sectional area of wire

> [!note] Current Density and Electron Current
> $$\begin{align*}
J &= \frac{I}{A} \quad \rightarrow\quad\frac{\text{current}}{\text{area}} \\\\
I &= ei_{e} \\\\
i_{e} &= n_{e}v_{d}A \\\\
J &= e n_{e} v_{d}
\end{align*}$$
