# Current Density
- *The amount of current per area of wire*

- Symbol : $J$

- Units : $\frac{I}{A}$ where:
	- $I$ : Current ($C$)
	- $A$ : Cross-sectional area of a wire ($m^2$)

> [!note] Current Density and Electron Current
> $$\begin{align*}
J &= \frac{I}{A} \\\\
I &= ei_{e} \\\\
i_{e} &= n_{e}v_{d}A \\\\
J &= e n_{e} v_{d}
\end{align*}$$
