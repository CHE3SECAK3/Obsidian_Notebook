# DeMorgan's Law


## Boolean Algebra
$$\overline{(x \cdot y)} = \overline{x} + \overline{y}$$
$$\overline{(x + y)} = \overline{x} \cdot \overline{y}$$

## Discrete Math
$$\neg{(p \land q)} = \neg{p} \lor \neg{q}$$
$$\neg{(p \lor q)} = \neg{p} \land \neg{q}$$
