# Electric Field of a Rod
$$\begin{align}\vec{E}_{\text{finite rod}} &= \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_{0}}\frac{Q}{r\sqrt{r^{2}+(\frac{L}{2})^{2}}}\\ \\
\vec{E}_{\text{infinite rod}} &= \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_{0}}\frac{2\lambda}{r}\end{align}$$
