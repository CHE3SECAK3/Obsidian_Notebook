# Conductance
- *Ability to conduct the flow of current*
- Symbol: $G$
- Units: Siemen ($S$)

- $G = \large\frac{1}{R}$
	- $G$ : Conductance ($S$)
	- $R$ : Resistance ($\Omega$)
	- $\therefore 1S = \large \frac{1}{\Omega} = \frac{1}{\frac{V}{A}} = \frac{A}{V}$
